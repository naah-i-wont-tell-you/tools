Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Comes First

At XTPdf, your privacy is not just important—it's guaranteed by design.

No File Uploads

All processing happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly and modern web technologies. Your files never leave your device—they're not uploaded to any server, anywhere.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your documents or files
  • Personal information
  • Browsing activity
  • Usage patterns
  • Device information

No Accounts Required

You do not need to sign up, log in, or create an account to use XTPdf. Just visit our website and start using the tools immediately.

No Tracking

We do not use:

  • Cookies (except essential ones for the website to function)
  • Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • Third-party trackers
  • Social media pixels
  • Advertising networks

Your CPU Power

Processing uses your device's CPU—no server involvement

Works Offline

Once loaded, tools work without internet connection

Open Standards

Built with modern web standards and open source libraries

Our Privacy Guarantee

We don't see or store anything you do. Everything stays on your device.

Contact Information

Email: xtpdfcustomercare@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the changes on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Since we don't collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.
